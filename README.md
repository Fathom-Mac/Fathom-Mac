## рџ“‹ Overview

<a href="#">Fathom</a> addresses the documentation reliability problem that affects meeting-heavy organizations: human memory degrades quickly after calls end, notes taken during meetings reflect divided attention and incomplete capture, and the records that do get produced often represent the note-taker's selective attention rather than a comprehensive account of the conversation.

The <a href="#">CRM automatic sync</a> pushes meeting summaries, discussion highlights, and action item lists to Salesforce, HubSpot, and other connected CRM systems immediately after calls with associated contacts. Sales teams capture conversation details without manual data entry, producing pipeline records that reflect actual customer conversations rather than what was entered from memory later. <a href="#">Team record distribution</a> shares meeting recordings and AI summaries with colleagues who could not attend, delivering a complete account rather than a secondhand recollection.

<a href="#">Template-structured summaries</a> configure the format and sections of AI-generated documentation according to team-specific frameworks вЂ” discovery call structures, project review formats, one-on-one templates вЂ” ensuring the same meeting type consistently produces the same documentation structure rather than variable output. <a href="#">Post-meeting email composition</a> drafts follow-up messages summarizing decisions and action items using transcript content as source material, ready for review and sending without writing from memory.

The <a href="#">multi-language transcription support</a> handles meetings conducted in major languages, accommodating international team calls and conversations that include participants speaking in their native language. <a href="#">Recording and consent configuration</a> controls participant notification behavior, recording activation conditions, and data storage settings to meet the consent requirements and compliance policies of different organizational and jurisdictional contexts.

<summary>Can the AI-generated summary be edited before it is shared?</summary>

Yes. Every generated summary is fully editable before distribution. Review, add context, or correct any element before sending to colleagues or pushing to CRM systems.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Does Fathom record in-person meetings as well as video calls?</summary>

Fathom is optimized for video conferencing platforms. Recording in-person meetings through a device microphone is technically possible but lacks named speaker attribution and platform integration features available for video calls.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How do custom summary templates work?</summary>

Configure section headings and content structure for specific meeting types вЂ” sales calls, project reviews, one-on-ones. Fathom generates AI summaries consistently following that structure for the designated meeting type.

</details>
